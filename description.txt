1.0.8 Changelog:
    - Hardmode metal buffs (except axes and repeaters) are now only active if Calamity is loaded.

Adds a bunch of new weapons to supplement progression as well as some buffs to vanilla gear.

Buffs:
    - If Calamity is loaded:
        - Hardmode metal swords have their damage increased by 35%
        - Hardmore metal spears and the Dark Lance have their damage increased by 40%
    - Hardmore metal and Hallowed repeaters have their damage increased by 25%
    - Hardmore metal axes have their damage doubled
    - Chlorophyte swords, shotbow, axe, hammer, drill, and pickaxe buffed
    - Starfury, Flintlock, Brand of the Inferno, Flying Dragon, and Phantom Phoenix buffed
    - Pearlwood armor significantly buffed, gives increased life regen, max health, and max mana
